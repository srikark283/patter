§ Everything on-device

I

On-device transcription

OpenAI Whisper and Nvidia Parakeet, Metal-accelerated. Six models from 78MB to 1.6GB — pick fast or accurate.

II

AI cleanup

A local Ollama model fixes grammar and strips filler words before the text is typed. Silence trimmed by on-device VAD.

III

Meeting notes

Record a meeting, get timestamped speaker-labeled transcripts plus minutes, decisions, and action items.

IV

Personal memory

Teach Patter facts about you and your work. It gets your names, projects, and jargon right — matched with local embeddings.

V

Voice macros

Say a short trigger phrase, get a whole block of text — addresses, sign-offs, boilerplate — as you dictate.

VI

Push-to-talk

Toggle with a hotkey or hold-to-record, even a bare modifier key. Types into any app, right where your cursor is.

App-aware profiles

Patter knows what app you're in — and writes accordingly

Slack & Messages Casual, contractions kept, minimal punctuation
Code editor Precise, technical terms preserved verbatim
Mail Formal, full sentences, greeting/sign-off aware
Notes Light cleanup only — your voice, tidied

Each profile is a set of cleanup rules — punctuation, formality, how aggressively filler words get trimmed. Assign once, Patter remembers.

How it works

Three steps to never typing again

01

Press your hotkey

Anywhere on your desktop — F12 by default, or hold a key push-to-talk style. A minimal HUD shows you’re live.

02

Say it

Whisper or Parakeet transcribes on your GPU; your local LLM polishes the result using your memory and app profile.

03

It’s typed

Text appears at your cursor in whatever app you’re in. Every dictation lands in History, editable and searchable.

Install

One command, no dialog drama

Terminal
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/srikark283/patter/main/install.sh | sh

Installs the latest release to /Applications. Then launch from Spotlight — onboarding walks you through mic access, a speech model, and your hotkey.

Prefer downloading the DMG yourself?
Grab it from the latest release, drag Patter to Applications, then clear the browser's quarantine flag — Patter is an indie app without Apple notarization, so macOS calls the download "damaged" until you run xattr -cr /Applications/patter.app. The curl route skips this entirely. First install only.
Prefer downloading the installer yourself?
Grab the .exe or .msi from the latest release and run it. Patter isn't EV-signed, so Windows SmartScreen may show "Windows protected your PC" — click More info → Run anyway. The PowerShell route skips this entirely. First install only.
