Everything on-device

A complete voice workflow, not just dictation

🎙️

On-device transcription

OpenAI Whisper and Nvidia Parakeet, Metal-accelerated. Six models from 78 MB to 1.6 GB — pick fast or accurate, switch anytime.

✨

AI cleanup

A local Ollama model fixes grammar and strips filler words before the text is typed. Silence trimmed by on-device VAD so noise never becomes words.

👥

Meeting notes

Record a meeting, get timestamped speaker-labeled transcripts plus minutes, decisions, and action items. Rename speakers, edit anything.

🧠

Personal memory

Teach Patter facts about you and your work. It uses them to spell your names, projects, and jargon right — matched on-device with local embeddings.

⚡

Voice macros

Say a short trigger phrase, get a whole block of text — addresses, sign-offs, boilerplate. Expansion happens as you dictate.

⌨️

Push-to-talk

Toggle with a hotkey or hold-to-record — even a bare modifier key. Types into any app, right where your cursor is.

🎯

App-aware tone

Per-app cleanup profiles: casual in Slack, precise in your editor. Patter knows which app you're dictating into.

📊

Your numbers

Words dictated, time saved, transcription latency — a dashboard of everything, stored only on your disk.

How it works

Three steps to never typing again

01

Press your hotkey

Anywhere in macOS — F12 by default, or hold a key push-to-talk style. A minimal HUD shows you're live.

02

Say it

Whisper or Parakeet transcribes on your GPU; your local LLM polishes the result using your memory and app profile.

03

It's typed

Text appears at your cursor in whatever app you're in. Every dictation lands in History, editable and searchable.

Install

One command, no Gatekeeper drama

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/srikark283/patter/main/install.sh | sh

Installs the latest release to /Applications. Then launch from Spotlight — onboarding walks you through mic access, a speech model, and your hotkey. Updates install themselves in-app.

Prefer downloading the DMG yourself?
Grab it from the latest release, drag Patter to Applications, then clear the browser's quarantine flag — Patter is an indie app without Apple notarization, so macOS calls the download "damaged" until you run xattr -cr /Applications/patter.app. The curl route skips this entirely (only browsers attach the flag).
